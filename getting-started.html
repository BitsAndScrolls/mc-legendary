Getting Started with Legendary Edition

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Notice: This modpack significantly increases Minecraft's difficulty. Dragons, legendary creatures, and enhanced survival mechanics make this a challenging experience even for veteran players.

üíæ Installation

CurseForge Installation

Download CurseForge App

Download and install the CurseForge app from curseforge.com

Import Modpack

Click "Create Custom Profile" and import the Legendary Edition modpack file (.zip)

Allocate RAM

Set memory allocation to at least 6GB (8GB recommended) in the profile settings

Launch & Play

Click Play and wait for the initial mod loading process to complete

Manual Installation

1. Install Minecraft Forge 1.20.1 (version 47.2.0 or higher) 2. Download all mod files from the provided list 3. Place all .jar files in your mods folder 4. Launch Minecraft with Forge profile 5. Configure as needed
üö® Warning: Manual installation is complex and error-prone. We strongly recommend using a modpack launcher.

üë∂ Your First Steps

üí° Tip: Press 'G' to open your skill menu and 'K' to view all keybindings when you first spawn.

Immediate Priorities (First 10 Minutes)

Check Your Skills

Open the skill menu (G key) and understand that you're limited by your skill levels. You can't use most tools/weapons until you level up!

Gather Basic Resources

Punch trees and gather wood. This gives you Gathering XP which is essential for progression.

Craft Wooden Tools

Make wooden tools immediately - they have no skill requirements and you'll need them to start mining.

Find Shelter

Night is much more dangerous with aggressive creatures. Find or dig a temporary shelter before dark.

üõ°Ô∏è Essential Survival Tips

üåô

Night Safety

Many new aggressive mobs spawn at night. Stay underground or in a well-lit, enclosed area until you have proper equipment.

üèÉ

Retreat Strategy

Always carry blocks for emergency shelters and food for healing. Running away is often the best strategy early game.

üó∫Ô∏è

Avoid Danger Zones

Stay away from large structures, dragon roosts, and areas with ominous sounds until you're properly equipped.

üìö

Read Everything

Find manuscripts in structures and craft a bestiary to learn about creatures. Knowledge is survival.

üö® Major Threats to Avoid Early Game:
  • Dragon Roosts: Large structures with dragons - instant death for new players
  • Legendary Creatures: Scarecrows appear when breaking crops, Hounds in forests
  • Large Dungeons: Wait until you have iron+ gear before exploring
  • The Graveyard Structures: Cursed areas with powerful undead

üìà Understanding the Skill System

The Reskillable mod locks equipment and abilities behind 8 different skills. You must spend XP to level these skills up.

The 8 Skills

‚õèÔ∏è

Mining

Unlocks pickaxes and mining tools. Level 8 = Iron Pickaxe, Level 16 = Diamond Pickaxe

üõ°Ô∏è

Defense

Unlocks armor. Level 12 = Iron Armor, Level 16 = Diamond Armor

‚öîÔ∏è

Attack

Unlocks weapons including Spartan Weaponry items. Essential for combat builds.

üîÆ

Magic

Unlocks magical items and spellcasting equipment. Level 12 = Enchanting Table

‚úÖ XP Efficiency Tips:
  • Mining XP: Strip mine and break lots of stone blocks
  • Attack XP: Fight mobs with weapons (not fists)
  • Defense XP: Take damage while wearing armor
  • Magic XP: Cast spells and use magical items

üéØ Recommended Progression Path

Days 1-3: Foundation Phase

  • Focus entirely on Mining until level 8 (Iron Pickaxe)
  • Build basic shelter and gather resources
  • Avoid all dangerous areas and creatures
  • Craft wooden then stone tools as permitted

Days 4-7: Equipment Phase

  • Level Defense to 12 for iron armor
  • Level Attack to 8 for better weapons
  • Continue mining for iron and materials
  • Explore small structures cautiously

Days 8-14: Specialization Phase

  • Choose your build focus (Combat/Magic/Balanced)
  • Push toward diamond-tier equipment (level 16)
  • Start tackling enhanced dungeons
  • Begin magic studies if going magic route

Days 15+: Advanced Phase

  • Hunt legendary creatures with proper gear
  • Explore major dungeon complexes
  • Begin dragon hunting preparation
  • Master your chosen specialization

‚ö†Ô∏è Major Dangers to Know

üö® Immediate Threats: These can kill you instantly if unprepared
üêâ

Dragons (Ice & Fire)

  • Fire Dragons: Breathe fire, found in most biomes
  • Ice Dragons: Freeze attacks, found in cold areas
  • Lightning Dragons: Electrical attacks, nocturnal
  • Avoid completely until late game!
üëπ

Legendary Creatures

  • Scarecrows: Appear when breaking crops
  • Hounds: Forest creatures that immobilize
  • Desert/Forest Mojos: Spawn from plants
  • Scorpions: Poison damage in deserts
üè∞

Legendary Monsters

  • 9 boss-type creatures with unique lairs
  • Found in special structures
  • Drop powerful unique weapons
  • Require endgame preparation
ü™¶

Graveyard Structures

  • Cursed areas with undead
  • Powerful enemies and traps
  • Great loot but extreme danger
  • Avoid until well-equipped
